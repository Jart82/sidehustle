Dr. Moses.J.B. Agarin

A man with a golden heart

Here's some thing's about Dr. Agarin:

Born July 14. Became an orphan at 12, losing his dad at age 9 and his mom when he was 12.

Passed through school with the help of some scholarship schemes. Studied Medicine at the University Of Lagos.

Married to Mrs. Kayeth.M. Agarin, and has four kids.

Worked as at Kwale and Orerokpe etc. He was the the chief medical officer at Orerokpe before retiring and has trained and worked with many Doctors.

Dr. Agarin is a intelligent hardworking person that always cares for patients health before thinking of finanace.
